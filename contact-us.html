Frequently Asked Question

What is the Pistis Retech Fund?

Pistis’ Retech Fund is an investment product that currently pays a 2.75% annual return (interest paid monthly) and generates that return by investing in a diversified network of certified-community lenders that increase economic inclusion and drive capital into financially-underserved communities across America.
‍
To learn more about the specific terms of the investment you should review the Offering Circular, if you are an accredited investor looking to invest in the Flagship Fund you should review the Subscription Agreement.

What is the legal structure of my ReTech Fund investment?

When you invest in the Pistis ReTech Fund, you are issued Debentures that are offered to eligible investors pursuant to Section 183 of the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 1990, as amended.

What is the Pistis Retech Fund?

Our Debentures pay interest at a current rate of 2.5% per annum, compounded monthly, fixed for the duration of the notes. Management may change the interest rate from time-to-time, in a range from 2.5% to 3.5% per annum.
‍
However, any change in interest rates will only apply to CNote Notes issued on or after such increase and do not apply to CNote Notes issued prior to any such change. Interest rate changes are at the sole discretion of CNote.
‍
An investor may withdraw up to 10% of the investor’s principal and accrued, but unpaid, interest each quarter, generally upon 30 days’ notice and subject to available funds from loans to our CDFI partners and other cash available to the Company. Management retains discretion to allow investors to withdraw additional amounts, subject to the availability of additional funds.
